    Codevember Challenge - 2018

    Codevember is a challenge for coders to create something new during the month of November. This year's theme is JUKEBOX. Using code, create anything that comes to mind when you think of that word. Feeling up for the challenge? Click the link below to learn more, and join a community of creative coders this November!

  • The Rules

    • Be Open

      All of your project's code must be open sourced and shared publically, by November 30th, for anyone to replicate.

    • Fly Solo

      One project per person. However, you are allowed to work in a team of 2... as long as one person is new to coding.

    • Write Code

      Whatever you decide to make must have at least one line of code. There are no other restriction though.

    • Stick to the Theme

      You can make whatever you imagine, as long as you can explain how you derived the idea from from the theme.

  • Join a community of creative coders this Codevember!

    Formally accecpt the challenge by creating an account in the Friends Worldwide Slack space, and joining the #codevember channel.
