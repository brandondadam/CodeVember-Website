Every November coders come together as a community of creatives to take on the CodeVember challenge. Your challenge, should you choose to accept it, is to code anything you can imagine that fits with the theme in 30 days. This year's theme is JUKEBOX.

The Rules

Fly Solo

One person per project (unless you're new to code).

Write Code

You must write at least one line of code for your project.

Stick to the Theme

You can make anything, even an airplane, as long as you can explain how you derived the idea from the theme (Jukebox).

Be Open

All your code must be open sourced by the end of the month and publicly shared on github.

Join the Community

Formally accept the challenge by creating an account in the Friends WorldWide Slack space, and joining the #codevember channel.

All projects must be complete by Friday, November 30th. On that day, challenges will gather in their communities and join a big Google Hangout to connect with challengers from accross the globe. We'll all meet, and talk about our projects. More details on this will be released in the #codevember slack channel. So be sure to click the button below to accept the challenge!
