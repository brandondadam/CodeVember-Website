CodeVember is a challenge for coders to create something new during the month of November, alongside a community of other creative coders. This yearâ€™s theme is Jukebox. The point is to use code to create anything that comes to mind when thinking of that word. Feeling up for the challenge?

The Rules

If you choose to accept this challenge, you must follow these 4 simple rules...

Fly Solo

No teams, everyone must work on their own project (Ext: Teams of two are allowed, if one member is new to code).

Write Code

Whatever you make is totally okay, as long as it consists of, at least, one line of code.

Stick to the Theme

You can make an airplane, as long as you can exlpain how you derived the idea from this year's theme (Jukebox).

Be Open

All your code must be open sourced by the end of the month and shared publically. Feel free to add your projects under the CodeVember-Challenge on github.

Join the Community

Formally accept the challenge by creating an account in the Friends WorldWide Slack space, and joining the #codevember channel.

All projects must be complete by Friday, November 30th. On that day, challenges will gather in their communities and join a big Google Hangout to connect with challengers from accross the globe. We'll all meet, and talk about our projects. More details on this will be released in the #codevember slack channel. So be sure to click the button below to accept the challenge!
